Origins in Ancient Times

Ancient flatbreads: The concept of pizza began with ancient civilizations like the Greeks, Egyptians, and Romans, who baked flatbreads topped with oils, herbs, and local ingredients.

Pizza in Italy (Naples)

Modern pizza as we know it originated in Naples, Italy in the 18th and 19th centuries.Poor Neapolitans began adding tomatoes (a New World crop) to flatbread. It became a cheap, delicious street food for workers and families.

Pizza Margherita (1889)

According to legend, in 1889, pizza maker Raffaele Esposito created a pizza for Queen Margherita of Savoy.He topped it with tomatoes (red), mozzarella (white), and basil (green) to represent the Italian flag.This pizza was named Pizza Margherita and became iconic.
